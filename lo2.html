Learning goal

1.Poster - Apply Gestalt principles in design through an iterative poster creation process.

2.Paper Prototype HobIT - Develop and iterate on paper prototypes through user testing and feedback.

3.Figma Prototype HobIT - Develop and iterate on interactive prototypes through user testing and feedback.

4.Figma Prototype of PEP - Develop and iterate on high-fidelity prototypes using Figma through user testing and feedback.

Action

1.Poster - Design and iterate on a poster to demonstrate Gestalt principles. Me and my team used figma so we can make a poster. Learning about the mothod then combining our ideas in one gave us two posters.

2.Paper Prototype HobIT - Define the user flows and interactions for the project Create low-fidelity paper prototypes or wireframes Conduct usability testing with the paper prototypes Iterate and refine the prototypes based on user feedback

3.Figma Prototype HobIT - Create interactive prototypes and conduct user testing for the project, Conduct user testing or stakeholder feedback sessions with the prototypes Iterate and refine the prototypes based on the feedback received

4.Figma Prototype of PEP - Define the user flows and interactions for the portfolio ,create high-fidelity prototype using Figma ,conduct user testing or stakeholder feedback sessions with the prototypes ,iterate and refine the prototypes based on the feedback received

Research

3.Figma Prototype HobIT - After making the paper prototype we tested it and added more details. When it was done the tasks were already devided so everyone started doing their page and later on commenting on the overall result.

Reflection

Poster - After making the poster we got a feedback that the second poster the method is not very clear so my reflection is that I have to pay attention and to test my work more.

Paper Prototype HobIT - It was important to start with the wireframes and paper prototype first because it was easy to make changes. Our teacher when we had a lecture said that its really possible when you make something and are proud of it but then you show it to your boss and he says that its not good enough so you spent a lot of time and effort into it just so you can make it again diffrently, but with paper prototype you can erase and draw again.

Figma Prototype HobIT - When we made the figma prototype there were of course still changes but mostly small on colors. My reflection on this is that I have to look at the bigges picture so in future I can make better products.

Figma Prototype of PEP - I have the same reflection as the HobIT prototype.
